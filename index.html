Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

The short version

Pickr stores every decision you make on your device. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal data. We do not track you across apps or websites.

What data we store

Every decision you create — including the title, options, mode, and result — is saved locally on your device using Apple's secure storage. Your data never leaves your phone unless you explicitly share a result.

What we do not collect

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any identifier. We do not use analytics tools. We do not include any third-party trackers or advertising SDKs.

Subscriptions

If you subscribe to Pickr Pro, Apple handles the entire payment process. We receive only the information required to verify your subscription is active. We do not see your payment details.

Sharing results

When you tap Share on a decision result, iOS opens the standard share sheet. Any data transmitted from that point is controlled by the app you share to (Messages, Mail, etc.) and is subject to their privacy practices.

Children

Pickr is rated 4+ and safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change how the app handles data, we will update this policy and the date at the top of this section. Continued use of the app after an update means you accept the revised policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email cangokcel@hotmail.com.
